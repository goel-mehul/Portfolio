Projects Completed

Over the span of two years, I have worked on numerous projects geared towards my interest in solving world problems so as to think of potential solutions as well as to dig deeper into the topis

Shiny App for Homeless statistics

This shiny app is created to analyze the number of homeless people in the US. After my analysis, I found why some states like California and Florida have a higher rate of homelessness when compared to other states. I also worked on finding potential solutions and non-profit organisations that are working towards helping reduce the number of homeless people. The app contains visualizations of datasets to spread awareness about the continuous increments in homelessness.

EcoKeen - Carbon Footprint Calculator App

This figma app is created to help users become aware about the dangers of increasing carbon footprint as well as to calculate their daily carbon footprint amounts and help them set carbon footprint goals. The app uses a secondary device to calculate the amount of carbon ootprint that gets emmitted from a user's house and their lifestyle and automatically presents them on the app for the user to keep track on. The app also provides personalized solutions to help users work on reducing their carbon footprint levels.

ProSoccerStats - Soccer Statistics App

This app is created to help users keep track of their favourite soccer players. The app uses an API to generate continuous stats about every prominent soccer player. An user can go through the app to find statistics. The app also incorporates features like compare. This allows a user to compare two to four different players at a time. Finally, a user can also create an account and log in to the app to selcet their favourite players and make their dream teams.

HealthEase - HealthCare App

This app is created to help users keep track of their health. The app is specifically designed towards old people using large buttons and more images rather than text. The app has features where a user can monitor which medicines to take. It also asks users how they are feeling and if a person is feeling pain, they can communicate through it. The app also has a mental health side where it provides self-care options. Finally the app has notification option to notify all your emergency contacts along with the hospital and police if there is an emergency.
